01 — Web Platform · 2024
↑ 60% reduction in coordination overhead

Logistics Operations Dashboard

A mid-sized logistics company was running their entire operation through WhatsApp groups and spreadsheets. We built a full internal platform with real-time shipment tracking, driver assignment, route management, and automated client reporting — all role-based and mobile-ready.

The result: full operational visibility, no more manual status updates, and a 60% drop in coordination overhead within the first month.

ReactNode.jsPostgreSQLWebSocketsVercel
02 — AI Tool · 2024
↓ Review time from 4h to 12 minutes

Document Intelligence Tool

A legal services firm needed to extract key clauses, obligations, and dates from hundreds of contracts every month. We built an AI-powered document parser using GPT-4 with structured output — wrapped in a clean review interface that let their team validate results in seconds rather than hours.

The pipeline handles German and English contracts, flags ambiguous clauses, and exports structured data directly into their case management system.

PythonOpenAI APIFastAPIReactPostgreSQL
03 — Automation · 2023
↓ 400+ manual entries eliminated per month

Invoice Processing Pipeline

An e-commerce brand was manually entering every incoming invoice into their ERP — a slow, error-prone process consuming hours each week. We automated the full flow: email ingestion → OCR → data extraction → validation → ERP sync, with a human review step for edge cases only.

The system handles 400+ invoices per month and reduced processing errors to near zero.

PythonOCRn8nREST APIERP Integration
04 — Web App · 2023
→ Zero phone bookings needed after launch

Self-Service Booking Platform

A service business was processing all bookings manually over the phone — unscalable as they grew. We built a fully self-service booking platform with Google Calendar sync, automated SMS and email reminders, Stripe payments, and a simple admin panel for their team.

Within two weeks of launch, 100% of bookings moved online. Their team got back hours each week.
