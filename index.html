01 — Web Platform · 2024
↑ 60% reduction in coordination overhead

Logistics Operations Dashboard

A mid-sized logistics company running their entire operation through WhatsApp groups and spreadsheets. We built a full internal platform with real-time shipment tracking, driver assignment, route management, and automated client reporting — role-based and mobile-ready.

The result: full operational visibility, no more manual status updates, and a 60% drop in coordination overhead within the first month.

ReactNode.jsPostgreSQLWebSocketsVercel
02 — AI Tool · 2024
↓ Review time from 4h to 12 minutes

Document Intelligence Tool

A legal services firm needed to extract key clauses, obligations, and dates from hundreds of contracts monthly. We built an AI-powered document parser using GPT-4 with structured output — wrapped in a clean review interface so their team validates results in seconds.

Handles German and English contracts, flags ambiguous clauses, and exports structured data directly into their case management system.

PythonOpenAI APIFastAPIReactPostgreSQL
03 — Automation · 2023
↓ 400+ manual entries eliminated / month

Invoice Processing Pipeline

An e-commerce brand manually entering every incoming invoice into their ERP — slow and error-prone. We automated the full flow: email ingestion → OCR → data extraction → validation → ERP sync, with human review for edge cases only.

The system handles 400+ invoices per month with processing errors reduced to near zero.

PythonOCRn8nREST APIERP Integration
04 — Web App · 2023
→ 100% of bookings moved online

Self-Service Booking Platform

A service business processing all bookings over the phone — unscalable as they grew. We built a fully self-service platform with Google Calendar sync, automated SMS and email reminders, Stripe payments, and a simple admin panel for their team.

Within two weeks of launch, zero phone bookings needed. Their team recovered hours every week.
